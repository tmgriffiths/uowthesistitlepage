cocordinates:
1sl9_solvate_ions_eq.pdb---Set up by Dr Haibo Yu. In TIP3 box side length 65 Å with Na and Cl ions to make neutral and physiological concentration. Residues 1-4, 123-127 were aded with MODLOOP to insert residues not seen in the X-ray structure.
    Three different starting structures through different amounts of minimisation on the above (conjugated gradient---NAMD default).
    SIM 1: 200 steps of minimisation.
    SIM 2: 0 steps of minimisation.
    SIM 3: 100 steps of minimisation. 

MOLECULAR MECHANICS
The 58 ns simulation was split into 1 ns segments the first 8 were used to bring equilibrate the system and the last 50 were free propagation.

Equilibration:
[0-4)ns
The system was restrained on all centres except the modelled loops (residues 1-4, 123-127) with a harmonic restraint.
consref: 1sl9_solvate_ions_eq_loop_cons.pdb
conskfile: 1sl9_solvate_ions_eq_loop_cons.pdb
force constant: 1 kcal.mol^1.Å^-1 
constraintScaling 8 (0 ns) 4 (1 ns) 2 (2 ns) 1 (3 ns)

[4-8)ns
The system was restrained on the protein and ligand with a harmonic restraint.
consref: 1sl9_solvate_ions_eq_cons.pdb
conskfile: 1sl9_solvate_ions_eq_cons.pdb
force constant: 1 kcal.mol^1.Å^-1 
constraintScaling 8 (4 ns) 4 (5 ns) 2 (6 ns) 1 (7 ns)
